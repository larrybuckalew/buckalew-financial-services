Medicare Insurance

Medicare is a federal health insurance program designed for people who are 65 or older, as well as some younger individuals with disabilities or specific health conditions.

Benefits of Medicare:

  • Comprehensive coverage for hospital stays, doctor visits, and preventive services
  • Prescription drug coverage through Medicare Part D
  • Affordable premiums and cost-sharing options

Medicare Coverage Options:

  • Original Medicare (Part A and Part B): Covers hospital stays, doctor visits, and preventive services.
  • Medicare Advantage (Part C): Offers an alternative way to receive your Medicare benefits through private insurance companies.
  • Medicare Prescription Drug Coverage (Part D): Helps cover the cost of prescription medications.
  • Medicare Supplement Insurance (Medigap): Provides additional coverage to help pay for out-of-pocket costs not covered by Original Medicare.
