Medicare Plan Options

Medicare Part A

Hospital Insurance covering inpatient stays, skilled nursing, hospice, and some home health care.

  • Covers hospital stays
  • Skilled nursing facility care
  • Some home health services

Medicare Part B

Medical Insurance covering outpatient care, preventive services, medical supplies, and physician services.

  • Outpatient care
  • Preventive services

Medicare Advantage (Part C)

Comprehensive plans that combine Part A, Part B, and often Part D coverage through private insurance companies.
