Navigating Medicare: What You Need to Know

Understanding Medicare Basics

Medicare is a federal health insurance program primarily designed for individuals 65 and older. Understanding its various parts is crucial for making informed healthcare decisions.

Medicare Parts Explained

  • Part A: Hospital insurance covering inpatient stays
  • Part B: Medical insurance for outpatient care and preventive services
  • Part C: Medicare Advantage Plans
  • Part D: Prescription drug coverage

Enrollment Tips

Key considerations for Medicare enrollment:

  1. Understand your initial enrollment period
  2. Compare different plan options
  3. Consider supplemental insurance
  4. Review your healthcare needs annually
