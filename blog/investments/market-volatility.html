Understanding Market Volatility: A Guide for Investors

Published: February 1, 2024 By Larry Buckalew

What is Market Volatility?

Market volatility refers to the rate at which the price of securities increases or decreases. High volatility means significant and rapid price movements.

Strategies for Navigating Volatility

  1. Maintain a Diversified Portfolio
  2. Keep a Long-Term Perspective
  3. Avoid Emotional Decision-Making
  4. Consider Dollar-Cost Averaging

Turning Volatility into Opportunity

While market fluctuations can be stressful, they can also present unique investment opportunities for the prepared investor.
